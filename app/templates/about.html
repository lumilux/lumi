Lumilux, a photoblog

Lumilux is a photoblog, or a regularly-updated collection of digital photography. The goal is to publish at least one photograph every few days, but I abandoned this goal long ago. The name “Lumilux” was inspired by the suggestions of Ray McKenzie. The first photo on Lumilux was published on September 16th, 2005.

Hans E Hyttinen, a photographer

I'm a developer at Turntable. Follow me on Twitter here.

Photos published—and not published—on Lumilux are sometimes posted to Flickr.

Lumi, a photoblog engine

The code behind Lumilux is available on GitHub.

In a previous life, Lumilux was powered by a heavily-modified version of Textpattern and was hosted on TextDrive.

Laurels, on which I rest

Lumilux was featured as one of "50 Wonderful, Inspiring Photoblogs" by Smashing Magazine on February 14, 2009.

Lumilux was accepted to WeLoveTxp.com on July 16, 2008.

Lumilux was featured on September 1, 2007 at Design Shack.

Lumilux was selected for the August 2007 gallery at CSS Container.

In August, 2007, Lumilux was accepted into CSS Glance.

Lumilux was a runner-up for Best Photoblog Design in the 2007 Photobloggies.

On May 23, 2007, Lumilux was inducted into the Photoblogs.org Hall of Fame in recognition of having "made a significant impact on the Photoblogs.org community".
